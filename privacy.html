Privacy Policy

Your data, your control, our commitment to excellence

Last Updated: August 4, 2025
Effective Date: August 4, 2025
Privacy-First Architecture: Zero data monetization guaranteed

1. Introduction

Welcome to Oneless, a revolutionary smoking tracker and behavioral coach designed to help you gradually reduce smoking through real-time insights and personalized guidance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Oneless is developed and operated by Ayon, based in the United Arab Emirates. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at xodaxoda@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Data You Directly Provide:

  • Account Information: Email address for account authentication and communication
  • Initial Setup Data: Your estimated daily cigarette consumption and cigarette pack price (stored in Firebase Realtime Database)
  • Smoking Behavior Data: Daily smoking logs including timestamps of smoking sessions, cigarette counts, and behavioral classifications (stored as backup files in Firebase Storage)
  • App Preferences: Theme settings (dark/light mode), shake sensitivity levels, and notification preferences

Data Collected Automatically:

  • Device Information: Unique Device ID for subscription management and device switching tracking
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app interaction patterns, feature usage statistics, session duration, and crash reports collected through Firebase Analytics
  • Technical Data: App version information, network connection status, and device performance metrics
  • Timing Data: Server timestamps for online connectivity checks and subscription validation

Data Storage Locations:

  • Device Storage: Current smoking data, app preferences, and offline connectivity check information
  • Firebase Realtime Database: Initial setup data (daily cigarette estimate and pack price)
  • Firebase Storage: Daily smoking logs and behavioral data as backup files
  • Firebase Authentication: Secure login credentials and account verification

3. How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality:

  • Analyze your smoking patterns to provide personalized behavioral insights and real-time feedback
  • Track your progress over time with daily, weekly, and monthly comparisons
  • Calculate financial savings based on your reduction progress using your pack price data
  • Generate automatic goals and coaching notifications based on your smoking behavior
  • Enable backup and restore functionality for your smoking data across app reinstallations

Account and Subscription Management:

  • Authenticate your account securely using Firebase Authentication
  • Manage subscription status and device limitations (one account per device)
  • Track device switching history to enforce the once-per-month device change policy
  • Validate subscription status during online connectivity checks
  • Enforce app version requirements for security and stability

Security and Anti-Fraud:

  • Detect potential time tampering by comparing device time with previous online checks
  • Monitor connectivity patterns to prevent subscription abuse
  • Freeze accounts that exceed offline limits (11 days) until online verification
  • Restrict app usage for outdated versions to maintain security standards

Communication and Notifications:

  • Send immediate notifications when you add or delete cigarette entries
  • Provide 2-hour activity reminders if you haven't logged recent smoking activity
  • Send offline reminders if you haven't been online for extended periods (up to 11 days)
  • Alert you about subscription status changes, including frozen subscription notifications
  • Notify you about required app updates and version compatibility issues

Analytics and App Improvement:

  • Collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics to improve app functionality
  • Analyze app performance, crash reports, and user interaction patterns
  • Monitor feature usage to guide development priorities
  • All analytics data is anonymized and cannot be traced to individual users

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with trusted service providers essential for app functionality:

Google Firebase Services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure login and account management
  • Firebase Realtime Database: Stores your initial setup data (cigarette estimate and pack price)
  • Firebase Storage: Encrypted backup storage for your daily smoking logs and behavioral data
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance and user experience

Google Play Services:

  • Google Play Billing: All subscription processing is handled entirely by Google Play
  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information
  • Subscription status and billing history are managed through your Google Play account

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

5. Data Security and Storage

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

Encryption and Security:

  • Data in Transit: All communication with our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Data at Rest: Your backup files in Firebase Storage are encrypted
  • Authentication: Secure Firebase Authentication with industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict limitations on who can access your personal data

Data Storage Architecture:

  • Local Device: Current smoking data, preferences, and offline check timestamps
  • Cloud Backup: Firebase Storage for daily logs, Firebase Realtime Database for setup data
  • No Automatic Sync: Data does not automatically sync between multiple devices
  • Manual Backup/Restore: Users must manually backup before switching devices

Data Retention Policy:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained indefinitely while your account remains active
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal data is permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request
  • Backup Data: Firebase Storage backups are deleted when you delete your account
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics data is retained according to Firebase Analytics policies

6. Device Management and Subscription Policies

Device Switching Policy:

  • First Switch: Unlimited device switches when you first create your account
  • Subsequent Switches: After your first device change, you must wait 1 month between device switches
  • Emergency Switches: Contact us at xodaxoda@gmail.com for earlier device switches with verification
  • Violation Consequences: Unauthorized device switching disables premium features until policy compliance

Account Security Measures:

  • Connectivity Requirements: Must connect online every 3 days for subscription validation
  • Offline Limit: Accounts are frozen after 11 days without online connectivity
  • Time Tampering Detection: Device time manipulation triggers security measures
  • Account Recovery: Frozen accounts can be restored through online connectivity check

App Version Control:

  • Version Checking: App version is verified during each subscription validation
  • Mandatory Updates: Outdated versions may be restricted from app usage
  • Security Purpose: Version control ensures security patches and critical fixes are applied

7. Your Data Rights and Control

Data Access and Management:

  • View Data: Access all your smoking data through the app's analytics dashboard
  • Edit Data: Modify or delete individual smoking entries directly in the app
  • Export Data: Request complete data export by contacting xodaxoda@gmail.com
  • Backup Control: Manually create and restore backups as needed

Account Deletion:

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

  • Email us at xodaxoda@gmail.com with your deletion request
  • We will remove all personal information from our systems within 30 days
  • This includes data in Firebase Realtime Database, Firebase Storage, and device records
  • You will receive confirmation email once deletion is complete

Notification Preferences:

  • Customize notification types and frequency in app settings
  • Disable specific notification categories (reminders, progress updates, etc.)
  • Control system-level notifications through your device settings

8. International Data Transfers and GDPR Compliance

Oneless is available worldwide. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States (where Firebase servers are located) and the European Union. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

European Union Users (GDPR Rights):

  • Right to Access: Request copies of all your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data in portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at xodaxoda@gmail.com with your specific request.

9. Children's Privacy

Oneless is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. We do not have automated age verification processes but rely on users to confirm they meet the minimum age requirement. If we discover we have collected information from someone under 18, we will delete that information immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, app features, or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes through the app notification system or via email. Your continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
